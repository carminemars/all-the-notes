Hey, it's Mars

Welcome to my personal docs. This is where I put everything my brain refuses to keep.

Who I am​

I'm a software developer with too many hobbies and a terrible memory.

I love learning. Not just the outcome, but the process: the digging, the trial-and-error, the late-night rabbit holes (well probably not), the moment something finally clicks (that's the point). The problem is that once it clicks, my brain files it somewhere in the depths and I have to start over next time.

This site is my way of making sure the journey doesn't reset to zero every few weeks.

My learning process​

I read, watch courses, take paper notes, then consolidate everything here. That last step is what makes it stick.

Once it's here, I can come back for details, refresh my memory, or dig deeper without starting from zero. And if sharing these notes helps someone else along the way, that's a nice bonus.

What you'll find here​

  • Tech - code, architectures, frameworks, tools, workflows
  • Music - theory, instruments, production tricks
  • Food - recipes, techniques, ingredients I keep forgetting
  • Sport - notes, analysis, things I picked up playing and watching
  • Other - whatever earns a page
