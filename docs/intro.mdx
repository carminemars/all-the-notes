# Hey, I'm Carmine ✌🏻

Welcome to my personal docs — the place where I store everything my brain stubbornly refuses to remember.

---

## Who I am

I'm Carmine, and I'm **passionate about a lot of things**. Maybe too many.

The world that fits best in a docs site is tech: code, frameworks, architectures, tools, workflows... and you'll definitely find plenty of that here. But hang on — I'm also the kind of person who gets into music, food, sport, and pretty much anything else that manages to hold my attention for more than five minutes.

The problem is that some of these passions come with a **serious learning curve** — and my memory, let's be honest, is pretty terrible.

So I stopped fighting it and started writing everything down.

---

## Why this site exists

Simple: I'm forgetful, curious, and a little obsessive when I get into something.

This docs is my **second brain** — a place to dump concepts, commands, notes, snippets, half-baked thoughts, things I learned at 2am and forgot by 9am.

It's not meant to be a perfect guide or a complete course. It's my very personal system for not starting from scratch every time I pick up a topic after three weeks away from it.

If something here is useful to you, great. If not, no worries — I wrote it for myself first.

---

## What you'll find here

- 🖥️ **Tech** — code, architectures, frameworks, tools and the whole circus
- 🎵 **Music** — theory, instruments, production, things I figured out the hard way
- 🍕 **Food** — recipes, techniques, ingredients I can never remember
- ⚽ **Sport** — notes, analysis, stuff learned from watching and playing
- 🎲 **Other** — anything that earned its place on a page
